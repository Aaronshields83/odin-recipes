Baked Salmon

Best Baked Salmon

  • 1 Salmon filet
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 10 sprigs of fresh rosemary
  • 2 large lemons
  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Evenly season salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and rosemary. Drizzle lemon juice from 1 lemon evenly over the fillet
  3. Slice 1 large lemon and evenly spread it over the fillet
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, 20 to 25 minutes

Per Serving: 127 calories; protein 20.7g; carbohydrates 1.3g; fat 3.8g; cholesterol 48.3mg; sodium 1214.3mg
